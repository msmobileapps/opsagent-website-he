Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to deploy OpsAgent?

OpsAgent can be deployed in under 30 minutes. Our quick-start setup includes guided configuration, pre-built templates, and 24/7 deployment support to get you operational immediately.

Which integrations are included?

OpsAgent integrates with 12+ major platforms including Slack, Jira, GitHub, Salesforce, Zoho, HubSpot, Microsoft Teams, and more. Custom integrations can be added based on your specific needs.

How is data security handled?

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard protocols. We comply with SOC 2, GDPR, and ISO 27001. Your data never leaves your infrastructure with our on-premise option.

Can I customize workflows for my team?

Yes! OpsAgent offers full workflow customization through our drag-and-drop builder, custom automation rules, and conditional logic engine. No coding required.

How does error handling work?

OpsAgent includes intelligent error detection, automatic retry logic, and detailed logging. Errors are captured, escalated, and can trigger custom notifications or rollback procedures.

What are the technical requirements?

OpsAgent works with most modern tech stacks. Cloud deployment requires minimal setup. On-premise deployment needs Docker/Kubernetes. Our team handles all technical requirements during onboarding.
